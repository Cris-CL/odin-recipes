Hummus

Delicious Hummus

Middle eastern dip, goes well with everything and is super easy and fast to cook

Ingredients

  • Chickpeas or garbanzo beans
  • Tahini
  • Lemon juice
  • Fresh garlic
  • Ground cumin and salt
  • Olive oil

Preparation

  1. If you use canned Chickpeas go straight to step 2 if not make sure that the Chickpeas are well cooked
  2. Add everything to the food processor little by little, salt, lemon juice and olive oil should be added depending on how the taste wanted
  3. Once everything is mixed put on a bowl and sprinkle with cumin and a little olive oil on the top for better look
