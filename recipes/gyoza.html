Gyoza

Delicious dumplings

Typical Japanese recipe of dumplings, usually served at izakaya with a glass of cold beer, life don't get that much better

Ingredients

  • gyoza/dumpling wrappers
  • 170 grams of ground beef and pork
  • 2 leaves of chinese cabbage
  • spring onion
  • garlic clove
  • fresh ginger root
  • 2 teaspoon of sake
  • 1 teaspoon of sesame oil
  • 1 teaspoon of soy sauce
  • sunflower oil

Preparation

  1. Chop finely all the vegetables
  2. Mix everything together with the meat until uniform consistence
  3. Put one tablespoon of the mix on the center of the wrapper
  4. With your finger put water on the edges of the wrapper
  5. Fold the Gyoza
  6. When you finish all the gyoza put them in a friyin pan with boiling oil
  7. After a momet when the gyoza are golden brown in the bottom pour 1/3 cup of water on the pan and close the lid
  8. Wait a moment until the top part is properly steamed and they are ready :)

Serve your gyoza with soy sauce and chili oil
